Beer Tools by Ruby Developement

Have you ever wanted to measure alcohol by volume but the eqution was to complicated? Well Ruby Development has simplified the process by making a calculator that does the work for you. With the Beer Tools app by Ruby Developement you just enter your original gravity and your final gravity and click calculate, then it give you your alcohol by volume. I told you it was simple, so simple even a baby could figure it out. The download link is below.
